## üÜò Troubleshooting

### "I can't remember my password"

If you've forgotten your password:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Follow the reset instructions sent to your email

### "I'm still seeing error messages"

If you continue to see authentication errors after logging in:

1. **Try a hard refresh:**
   - Windows/Linux: Press `Ctrl + Shift + R`
   - Mac: Press `Cmd + Shift + R`

2. **Clear your browser cache:**
   - Open your browser settings
   - Find "Clear browsing data"
   - Select "Cached images and files"
   - Click "Clear data"

3. **Try logging out and back in:**
   - Click on Settings
   - Click "Log Out"
   - Close the tab completely
   - Open AuroraLink again
   - Log in

### "Nothing is working"

If you're still having issues:

1. **Check the browser console** (for developers):
   - Press F12 to open Developer Tools
   - Look for error messages in red
   - Take a screenshot

2. **Contact Support:**
   - Provide your email address
   - Describe what you're seeing
   - Include any error messages
   - Include screenshots if possible
